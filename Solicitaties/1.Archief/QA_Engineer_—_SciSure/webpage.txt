The role
As a QA Engineer at SciSure, you are part of one of our product teams and take ownership of testing throughout the software lifecycle. You write and automate test cases, join sprint planning and refinement, and keep an eye on quality metrics such as test results and coverage. You work closely with developers and product owners and make sure the user’s perspective is included in our release decisions.
You help keep our CI/CD pipelines healthy, share best practices with the team, and look for practical ways to improve how we build and deliver our software.
