Work @PolyVation
For our customers world-wide we realize innovations by development, manufacture and supply of very special polymers. With our work we improve the quality of life of numerous patients. The polymers we make result in new breakthrough products for replacement of tissues, healing of tissues or treating chronic diseases by local controlled release of medicine.

Want to become part of our team?
PolyVation employs motivated, highly skilled people who like to be challenged every day. Many of our employees are educated at university or polytechnic level and are well-trained in polymer chemistry, organic chemistry, process engineering, quality engineering or analytics. Well-trained production engineers, laboratory support engineers and quality control technicans complete our team.
We work in an atmosphere of openness, respect and trust with a constant drive to provide our customers with the best possible polymers and services.
